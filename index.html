Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors Program

The Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors is a program to sponsor students majoring in disciplines related to technology. The MLSA program enhances students' employability by offering training in skills not usually taught in academia, including knowledge of Microsoft technologies.
This was a short definition from Wikipedia now let's talk more about my personal experience so I joined the program in January 2021 and it was just amazing, as a new computer science student it was hard for me to find out reliable online learning sources and now as a Microsoft Student Ambassador I get the chance to attend so many training offered by the program, to connect with a global community of students and to share my knowledge.
I definitely recommend every IT student to go check the MLSA website, I will give the link below, and to apply to join the program, amazing experience is waiting for you.
